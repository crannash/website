About Me

Hello, my name is Tate Darin and I am a second-year undergraduate student at Northwestern University pursuing a double major in physics and integrated science. I am currently studying the dynamics of structural protein domains with the Keten group, and am also starting a project on modeling the dynamics of light-driven molecular rotors with the Gingrich group. My research makes heavy use of molecular dynamics techniques, and I think it would be interesting to try to write a set of notes giving an introduction to statistical mechanics integrated with an introduction to molecular dynamics simulations.
